M&C Firewood Supplies has been a trusted local business for over 10 years. Based in Market Drayton, Shropshire, we supply premium kiln-dried hardwood logs, all sourced from the UK — nothing imported.

We deliver throughout Shropshire, Staffordshire, and Cheshire with free delivery within 10 miles of Market Drayton. We offer stacking services for a small fee — contact us to discuss!

We take pride in our reliability, quality products, and exceptional customer service. Our large Peugeot Boxer van ensures timely and safe delivery of your order.
