About Us

M&C Firewood Supplies is based in Market Drayton, Shropshire. We deliver premium kiln-dried hardwood logs, wonky logs, kindling nets and more across Shropshire, Staffordshire, and Cheshire.

All of our products are UK-sourced and kiln dried — nothing is imported. We’ve been firewood experts for over 10 years, providing seasoned advice and top-quality fuel.

We’re known for being very reliable and offering excellent customer service. Whether you're heating your home or stocking up for the season, you can count on us to deliver — on time, every time.

We offer free delivery within 10 miles of Market Drayton. We’re happy to deliver further, though a small fee may apply depending on the size of your order.

All deliveries are made in our Peugeot Boxer LWB van.
